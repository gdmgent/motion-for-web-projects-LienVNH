Coco Chanel Mademoiselle

Mademoiselle is a feminine perfume by Chanel. The scent was launched in 2001 and the fragrance was created by perfumers Jacques Polge and Olivier Polge. Top notes are aldehydes, orange, mandarin orange, neroli, bergamot and lemon; middle notes are jasmine, lily-of-the-valley, rose, ylang-ylang, iris, carnation and lily; base notes are sandalwood, amber, musk, civet, oakmoss, vanilla and cedar.
